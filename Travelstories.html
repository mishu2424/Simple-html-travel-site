Paris - Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower is one of the most iconic landmarks in the world. Located in the heart of Paris, it offers panoramic views of the city and is a must-visit for every traveler.

Rome - Colosseum

The Colosseum is an ancient amphitheater in Rome and a symbol of the Roman Empire. It once hosted gladiator contests and public spectacles.

New York - Statue of Liberty

The Statue of Liberty is a universal symbol of freedom. Located on Liberty Island, it welcomes visitors arriving by sea and is a cherished landmark of the United States.
